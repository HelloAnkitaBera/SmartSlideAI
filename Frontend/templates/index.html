Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How long does it take to generate a presentation?

Most presentations are generated in 20-30 seconds. Longer presentations may take up to 60 seconds depending on complexity and server load.

Can I edit the generated presentations?

Yes! Presentations are downloaded as fully editable PPTX files. You can modify text, colors, layouts, and add your own images in PowerPoint or any compatible software.

What topics can I create presentations about?

You can create presentations about almost any topic - business, technology, education, marketing, science, history, and much more. Our AI is trained on diverse knowledge domains.

How many slides can I generate?

You can choose between 5, 7, 10, or 15 slides depending on your needs. Each slide is professionally formatted with proper structure and content.

Are presentations original or plagiarized?

All content is uniquely generated by our AI based on your topic. While examples may reference well-known concepts, the presentation is original and unique to you.

What file format do I get?

Presentations are delivered as PowerPoint (.PPTX) files compatible with Microsoft PowerPoint, Google Slides, and other presentation software.
